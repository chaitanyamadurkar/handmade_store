✦ About Us

About Us

Floral Knot started with a simple love for creating flowers that could stay forever. What began as handmade blooms made with care slowly turned into a small dream filled with creativity, warmth, and meaningful gifting. Every bouquet, floral card, pot set, and tiny décor piece is handcrafted patiently — knot by knot — to bring emotions into something you can keep forever. We believe flowers are more than just gifts; they hold memories, feelings, and moments that deserve to last a little longer. At Floral Knot, we create everlasting floral pieces that add softness, happiness, and beauty to everyday life — all handmade with love and thoughtful details. 🌷✨
